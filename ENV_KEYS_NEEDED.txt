    1. ANTHROPIC_API_KEY   ← most important, powers everything

    3. OPENAI_API_KEY      ← Whisper transcription

    5. APIFY_API_TOKEN
    6. META_APP_ID + META_APP_SECRET

  To test payments:
    7. STRIPE keys

  To test RAG scripts:
    8. PINECONE keys
